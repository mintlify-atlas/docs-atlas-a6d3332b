# Welcome to Creative!

Creative! is a cross-platform desktop application that helps gamers review their gameplay and theorize about creative solutions to their everyday in-game problems. Review your gameplay, use the analysis tools to better visualize your thoughts, and export them as analysis files to keep or share with friends and coaches.

## How it works

Creative! offers two modes for working with your gameplay videos:

### Replay mode
Watch your gameplay videos with full playback controls. Navigate the timeline, pause at key moments, and get a feel for what you want to analyze.

### Analysis mode
Switch to analysis mode to unlock the full power of Creative!'s annotation tools:

- **Create breakpoints** at specific timestamps
- **Draw annotations** on video frames with customizable colors
- **Add text notes** to explain strategies or observations
- **Record voice commentary** for detailed walkthroughs
- **Trim video segments** to focus on specific plays
- **Export everything** as a portable .an file

## Built for gamers

Whether you're a competitive player analyzing your mistakes, a content creator planning video commentary, or a coach reviewing team performance, Creative! gives you the tools to capture your insights and share them effectively.
